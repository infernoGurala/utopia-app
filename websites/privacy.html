1. Introduction

UTOPIA ("the App") is developed by John Moses ("we", "our", "us"), based in India. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following when you use the App:

  • Name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • University name and academic details
  • Attendance records, timetables, and schedules
  • User-uploaded files, notes, documents, and voice recordings
  • Focus session times and habit tracking records
  • Device location data (for campus mapping and geofencing features)

3. How We Store Your Data

Your data is synced to secure cloud infrastructure provided by Firebase and Supabase. No data is shared with unauthorized or unofficial third-party servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage)
  • Supabase (database and backend services)

5. Advertising

The App is currently free with no advertisements. We plan to introduce ads approximately one month after the initial Play Store release. This policy will be updated before ads go live.

6. Your Rights
